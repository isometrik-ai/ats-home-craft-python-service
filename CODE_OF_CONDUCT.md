# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone. By participating in this project, you agree to:

1. Be respectful and inclusive; assume positive intent.
1. Use welcoming and inclusive language; avoid personal attacks or harassment.
1. Accept constructive feedback gracefully; focus on technical merit.
1. Respect privacy and confidentiality of shared information.
1. Follow project maintainers’ guidance and decisions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or disruptive conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Email: rahul@houseofapps.ai (or contact a maintainer directly)
- Provide: what happened, where, when, and any supporting context.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept as confidential as possible. Consequences may include warnings, temporary or permanent bans from participation.

## Scope

This Code applies to all project spaces, including issues, PRs, discussions, chat, and in-person events where the project is represented.
